Want Fresh Eggs?

Start with a coop!

The hen house requires plenty of ventilation, egg boxes, & a door that can be closed to secure your pets.

Be sure to have room for essintial supplies:
–food
–water
–rails for perching

What kind of chickens do you want?

Take your time to select the chickens that will produce the color & quantities of eggs that you desire. Some breeds will
produce more eggs than others. And the size the eggs can vary depending on the age and breed of the chicken. You may select
smaller breeds for pets if eggs are not important to you. Or larger birds if you desire meat birds.

Rewards for patience

Once your pullets have matured they will begin to lay eggs. The fresh eggs will complement any back yard
vegetable garden to make a delicious and healthy breakfast.

Poultry presents

Besides the fresh eggs dont be surprised if you notice that your chickens benefit your yard in additional ways:
–chickens eart weed seeds
–they are natural pest controllers
–chicken droppings are high in nitrogen and make a great additive to any garden or compost pile
